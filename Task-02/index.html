Dr Aamir Liaquat Hussain tied the knot with the 18-year old

Popular television host and PTI MNA Dr Aamir Liaquat Hussain tied the knot with the 18-year old, Syeda Dania Shah. The announcement came a few hours after his second wife, actor Tuba Aamir confirmed their divorce after much speculation. She revealed that the former couple had been separated for the past 14 months. He has been posting videos of his now-wife on Instagram for quite a while now. Hussain’s latest bride also took to Instagram to announce the marriage. She called the television personality and now husband her “childhood ideal.”
